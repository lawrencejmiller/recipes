Ingredients:
 -  8oz Coconut-Almond milk (unsweeetened)
 -  2 scoops Chocolate protein shake powder
 -  1Tbsp Raw Cacao powder
 -  2Tbsp Natural Peanut Butter
 -  60-80gr Strawberries (Frozen or Raw)
 -  2Tbsp Vanilla Greek Yogurt (Oikos triple zero is good) 
 -  1 cup Ice

Directions:
 1. Combine all in blender.
 2. Blend until smooth and enjoy!
